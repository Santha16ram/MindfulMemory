🧠 Why Memory Feels Hard

Your working memory can only hold 7±2 items at once (Miller's Law). Stress hormones like cortisol actively shrink the hippocampus — the brain's memory center — over time.

🧘 What Meditation Does

Just 8 weeks of mindfulness practice increases grey matter density in the prefrontal cortex and hippocampus. Studies show a 16% improvement in working memory capacity after consistent practice.

⚡ The 20-Minute Effect

A single 20-minute session reduces cortisol by up to 25%, increases alpha wave activity (associated with calm focus), and primes neural pathways for better encoding and retrieval.
